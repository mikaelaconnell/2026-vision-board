03 · Marathon Training Plan

The Training Roadmap

🏋️
Phase 1
Hyrox Build
March 15 – May 29, 2026
Mar–Apr: 2–3x/week functional training (rowing, wall balls, sled sim, farmers carries) + 3 easy runs/week.
May 1–23: Peak training — timed Hyrox simulations, 3 functional + 3 run sessions/week.
May 24–30: Taper week. Easy movement only. Race Day May 30.
Running base you build here carries directly into marathon training. Don't skip the long runs.
🏊
Phase 2
Half Ironman Base → Peak
June 1 – race day (Sep/Oct 2026)
June: Recovery from Hyrox + base building. Swim 2x/week, easy cycling, run 3x/week.
July–Aug: Build volume. Long ride Sunday, long run Saturday, swim 2x mid-week.
Note: NYC Marathon 16-week plan starts ~July 14 — your long runs serve double duty.
Pick a Sep race to keep the July–Aug overlap manageable. An Oct race compresses your NYC Marathon taper.
🧠
Phase 3 — The Big One
NYC Marathon — 16-Week Plan
July 14 – November 1, 2026
Weeks 1–6 (Jul 14 – Aug 24): Overlaps with Half Ironman training. Long runs are the priority. Target 35–45 miles/week by end of this block.
Weeks 7–10 (Aug 25 – Sep 21): Half Ironman taper/race in here. Keep running volume, reduce swim/bike post-race.
Weeks 11–14 (Sep 22 – Oct 19): Peak marathon training. 18–22 mile long runs. One 20-miler by Oct 5.
Weeks 15–16 (Oct 20 – Nov 1): Taper. Cut volume 40%. Sleep, nutrition, race prep.
Your Half Ironman fitness (aerobic engine + mental toughness) is a massive advantage going into marathon training. Trust the base.
🔄
Phase 4 — The Bridge
NYC → Tokyo Recovery + Rebuild
November 1 – December 14, 2026
Nov 1–14: Full rest. You just ran a marathon. No running for 10–14 days minimum.
Nov 15 – Dec 14: Easy rebuild. 20–30 miles/week, all easy pace. No workouts. Just getting the legs back under you.
Dec 15: Begin Tokyo 11-week targeted plan. You'll have marathon fitness — this is just sharpening, not building from zero.
You have ~18 weeks between NYC and Tokyo. That's tight but very doable at your fitness level. The key is not skipping the recovery window.
🌸
Phase 5
Tokyo Marathon — 11-Week Sharpening Plan
December 15, 2026 – March 1, 2027
Dec 15 – Jan 11: Rebuild quality. Add tempo runs and progressions back in. 35–45 miles/week.
Jan 12 – Feb 8: Peak block. One 20-miler around Jan 19. Marathon-pace long run Jan 26.
Feb 9 – Mar 1: Taper. 3-week taper, cut volume. Arrive Tokyo early, adjust for time zone.
Tokyo is famously fast and flat. With your NYC fitness still in your legs, this could be a PR attempt.
⚡ Key Overlap Warning: July–September

Half Ironman training and NYC Marathon training overlap for about 10 weeks. This is manageable — your Saturday long run serves marathon training, your Sunday long ride serves Ironman. Swim 2x mid-week. Do NOT add extra volume; let the workouts serve both goals. If it gets overwhelming, the Half Ironman drops to Tier 2 — NYC Marathon is the priority event.
